Makes: 4 portions
Prep time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 15 minutes

### Ingredients
- 2 red onions, diced
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1⁄2 red bell pepper, diced
- 2 tbsp tomato extract
- 200ml hot water
- 130ml coconut milk
- 2 400g cans cooked white beans
- 1⁄2 lime, juiced and zested
- Fresh coriander
- Vegetable oil for frying
#### Spices
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1⁄2 tsp tumeric powder
- 1⁄2 tsp kashmiri chilli powder
- Salt & pepper to taste
- 1⁄2 tsp brown sugar
- 1 1⁄2 tbsp amchur
- 


### Instructions
- Heat olive oil in a pan, sweat onions until translucent.
- Add garlic, fry for 1–2 minutes. Add spices, roast for 30 seconds.
- Stir in tomato paste, toast briefly. Add bell peppers and cook for 30 seconds.
- Deglaze with hot water, bring to a boil and simmer for 5–6 minutes.
- Add coconut milk and white beans, bring to the boil again, then simmer gently for 5–6 minutes.
- Season with brown sugar, lime zest, lime juice, salt & pepper.
- Top with coriander and serve with rice, naan or baked potatoes.
